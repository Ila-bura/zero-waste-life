Carrot Fritters

Ingredients

  • 1 large carrot, grated
  • 1 small shallot, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 tbsp fresh parsley, roughly chopped
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 25g plain white flour
  • Generous pinch of salt + black pepper

Method

Add the grated carrot to a tea towel and squeeze to remove excess water.

Place the grated carrot into a bowl with the remaining ingredients and mix to combine.

To a frying pan over medium heat, add 2 tbsp of oil.

Roll the mixture into a ball. Flatten and fry on each side until golden and crispy.
