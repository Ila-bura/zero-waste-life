Tips & tricks

  • Cook the food you already have before buying more
  • Jot down a list before you go shopping and stick to it!
  • Plan your meals with similar ingredients
  • Try to incorporate the entire ingredient when you cook
  • Freeze leftovers or donate to a local charity
  • Learn how to compost at home
