Did you know?


According to the United Nations, nearly half of all fruit and veg produced globally each year is wasted.

Learn some tips and tricks

  • Cook the food you already have in the house before buying more
  • Create a list before going shopping and stick to that list!
  • Plan your meals with similar ingredients
  • When you cook, try to incorporate the entire ingredient
  • Save and freeze leftovers or donate to a local charity
  • Compost! Don't know where to start? Look here
